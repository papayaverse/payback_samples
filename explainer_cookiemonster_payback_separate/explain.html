Welcome to Papaya Privacy

What is Papaya Privacy?

Papaya Privacy is your partner in controlling your online data. With CookieMonster, we protect your privacy by managing cookie banners for you. With Payback, we create a completely new, ethical, and transparent data-sharing market where you’re in control.

CookieMonster

Default Mode: Automatically opts you out of tracking cookies for maximum privacy.

Customizable: Adjust preferences anytime to allow certain cookies based on your needs.

Payback

Go beyond blocking cookies! Share your data ethically with trusted companies, decide who can access it, and get paid for your data.

How They Work Together

CookieMonster ensures your privacy by shielding you from today’s broken data-sharing system. Payback empowers you to participate in a better one where you decide what happens to your data.

Think of it like this:

  • CookieMonster: “No, I don’t want to share my data!”
  • Payback: “Yes, but only on my terms—and I get rewarded!”
